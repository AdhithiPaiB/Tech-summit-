THE EVENT

" Technology will not replace great teachers, but technology in the hands of great teachers can be transformational"

Bengaluru Tech Summit has always been a gathering foreground for all those who thrive on businesses that leverage technology. Over the last few years, the summit has become a global catalyst, introducing next generation innovators to the market place & shrinking borders to bring world technology to Indian soil. A conducive stage, where industry experts share their insights on technological advancements in varied sectors and where businesses create fruitful cross-linkages to further their growth in the realm of IT and BT.

"The science of today is the technology of tomorrow"

Karnataka is globally recognised as the IT and Bio capital of the country with Bengaluru being branded as the most dynamic city in the world. With the boundless support showered upon by the Government of Karnataka, the state and the city offer the most conducive ecosystem for startups to ideate, innovate and invent for a smarter future. The state also shelters the largest pool of R&D and educational institutions in the country helping to create a knowledge centric ecosystem, thus being reputed as the knowledge capital of the country. With research, ideations and innovations, the city of Bengaluru and the state of Karnataka have taken leaps ahead on the ladder of technology bringing the spotlight time and again back to India. This year, the city is getting ready to further shrink borders and bring bigger and better technologies to the Indian soil with the 22nd edition of it's flagship event - Bengaluru Tech Summit 2019. Organised by the Dept of IT & BT, Government of Karnataka, the event, since its inception, has been a strategic platform for industries, technologies and academia to understand and leverage the latest technological innovations for a smarter tomorrow.

Brace yourselves Bengaluru and get ready to be a part of an event that tingles your grey cells. Plan your participation and benefits from being an exhibitor, sponsor, presenter, delegate or a visitor to get a sneak peek into the future of technology in the country and across the world. Block your dates and get ready to be amazed!
